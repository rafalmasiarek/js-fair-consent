### Cookies and analytics

We use a consent-based analytics loader to ensure GDPR/ePrivacy compliance. Analytics scripts run **only after** you click “Accept” in the banner.

Before consent:
- No tracking cookies are set;
- Anonymous, non-identifying analytics only (where applicable);
- No data is transferred to third parties outside the EU.

After consent:
- Additional tools (e.g., Google Analytics, Hotjar, Matomo) may be activated;
- Cookies may be stored as required for measurement;
- You can withdraw consent at any time.
